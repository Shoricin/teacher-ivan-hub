movie Peaky Blinders: Narrative Tenses

Lesson 5: Describe dramatic scenes using Past Tenses

menu_book Action Vocabulary

  • Forbid (Forbade): Prohibir estrictamente.
  • Behave: Comportarse bien.
  • Point at: Señalar a alguien con el dedo.
  • Shout: Gritar.
  • Ruin: Arruinar o echar a perder un evento.

Scene 1: The Wedding Rules Read & Analyze

Tommy had gathered all his brothers in the kitchen because they were preparing for his wedding. Before the guests arrived, he had given them very strict orders about their behavior.

While he was pointing directly at them, he suddenly shouted, "No fighting!" He forbade any type of violence or betting. He knew they usually caused trouble. The brothers nodded silently while they were listening to his furious warnings.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

While Tommy (talk), Arthur (listen). Tommy was angry because somebody (fight) at the previous party.

menu_book Action Vocabulary

  • Execute: Ejecutar o asesinar bajo órdenes.
  • Grave: Tumba.
  • Kneel: Arrodillarse.
  • Interrupt: Interrumpir.
  • Fate: El destino (especialmente uno trágico).

Scene 2: In the Empty Field Read & Analyze

Tommy was smoking his final cigarette because he truly thought he was going to die. The assassins had taken him to an empty field and they had already dug his grave in the mud.

Just as he was kneeling on the ground, a sudden gunshot interrupted the execution. One of the men surprisingly killed his partners and saved Tommy's life. He had accepted his terrible fate, but luck was on his side.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

When the assassin (fire) his gun, Tommy (wait) for his death. The assassins (bring) shovels earlier.

menu_book Action Vocabulary

  • Threat / Threaten: Amenaza / Amenazar.
  • Pull out: Sacar algo (ej. sacar un arma del bolsillo).
  • Pin: El seguro o pasador de la granada.
  • Bluff: Farolear o mentir para intimidar.
  • Tense: Muy tenso o lleno de estrés.

Scene 3: The Negotiation Read & Analyze

Tommy walked into Alfie Solomons' office to force a deal. Alfie was shouting aggressively because he had discovered a betrayal.

Suddenly, while they were arguing, Tommy pulled out a grenade from his coat. He had planned this extreme trick in advance. As Tommy was holding the pin with his finger, the situation became incredibly tense. Alfie thought he was bluffing, but he wasn't.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

Alfie (yell) when Tommy (show) the weapon. Tommy (prepare) the deadly trap carefully.
