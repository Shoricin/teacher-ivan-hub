Lesson 5: Describe dramatic scenes using Past Tenses

menu_book Action Vocabulary

  • Tremble: Temblar (físicamente, como el agua en el vaso).
  • Break out: Escapar por la fuerza de un encierro.
  • Roar: Rugir.
  • Stare: Mirar fijamente, sin apartar la vista.
  • Terrified: Extremadamente asustado.

Scene 1: The T-Rex Attack Read & Analyze

It was raining heavily in the park. The children, Lex and Tim, were waiting inside the electric car because the power had failed minutes earlier. Suddenly, Lex noticed the water trembling in her cup.

While they were staring into the dark forest, the massive T-Rex broke out of its enclosure. It roared loudly. The children had never seen a real dinosaur so close before. Just as the dinosaur was looking through the glass, Lex grabbed a flashlight.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

When the T-Rex (attack), the kids (scream) inside the car. They (lose) their radio connection previously.

menu_book Action Vocabulary

  • Dodge: Esquivar un ataque o proyectil.
  • Rooftop: El techo o azotea de un edificio.
  • Lean back: Inclinarse hacia atrás.
  • Fire a gun: Disparar un arma.
  • Slow motion: Cámara lenta.

Scene 2: Bullet Time Read & Analyze

Neo and Trinity had arrived at the rooftop to save their friend. Before this moment, Neo had never believed he was "The One".

While Neo was pointing his gun at the Agent, the Agent suddenly fired his weapon. As the bullets were flying towards him, Neo leaned back and dodged them in impossible slow motion. He had been practicing in the simulation, but this was real. Finally, Trinity saved him.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

Trinity (shoot) the Agent while he (look) at Neo. Before that day, Neo (never / move) so fast.

  • Flee (Fled): Huir de un gran peligro.
  • Shatter: Romper en pedazos (el puente).
  • Whip: Látigo (el arma del Balrog).
  • Abyss: Abismo o pozo sin fondo.
  • Sacrifice: Sacrificar algo/alguien.

Scene 3: The Bridge of Khazad-dûm Read & Analyze

The Fellowship was fleeing through the dark mines because a terrible demon had awakened. They had been running for hours when they finally reached the narrow bridge.

While the others were crossing safely, Gandalf stopped. He had decided to sacrifice himself. He shattered the bridge with his staff. The monster fell into the abyss, but as it was falling, its fiery whip caught Gandalf's leg.

Practice: Fill in the correct tense

Frodo (cry) out when Gandalf (fall). Gandalf (tell) them to run before the monster arrived.
