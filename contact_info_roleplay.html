Wh- Questions & The verb To Be

rule Grammar Rules

To ask for contact information, we use What (for things/numbers) or Who (for people) + the verb To Be (is/are).

Wh- Word Verb To Be Subject Answer
What is your phone number? It is 555-1234.
What are their email addresses? They are alex@mail.com and...
Who is your emergency contact? She is my sister.

Role: The Athlete

You are registering for a competition at CrossFit Sucre. Complete your answers.

Coach Carlos Welcome to the gym! I need to register you. What is your full name?
You My name Alex Taylor.
Coach Carlos Great. What is your phone number?
You It 77123456.
Coach Carlos And what is your email address? We send the workout routines there.
You email address alex@mail.com.
Coach Carlos Perfect. Just one last question: Who is your emergency contact?
You My emergency contact my brother. His phone number 77987654.
