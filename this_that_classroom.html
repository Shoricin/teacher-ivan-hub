touch_app Classroom Pointers

Mastering "This" and "That" with Classroom Objects

THIS

pan_tool_alt
Near / Cerca (Singular)
Use THIS to point at ONE object that is very close to you (you can touch it).

• This is my desk.
• This pencil is yellow.

THAT

arrow_forward
Far / Lejos (Singular)
Use THAT to point at ONE object that is far from you (you have to walk to touch it).

• That is the whiteboard.
• That backpack over there is heavy.

school Classroom Vocabulary

Desk (Pupitre) Whiteboard (Pizarra) Notebook (Cuaderno) Pencil Case (Estuche/Cartuchera) Backpack (Mochila) Eraser (Goma de borrar) Scissors (Tijeras) Projector (Proyector)
