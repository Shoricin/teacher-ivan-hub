El museo no perdió 35,000 visitantes. El museo cayó *hasta* 35,000 visitantes.

In 2015, there were 50,000 visitors. However, this number decreased (by/to) 35,000 in 2020. This means it decreased (by/to) 15,000 visitors overall.

pie_chart Lesson 1: Pie Charts & Proportions

The "Respectively" Rule

Cuando reportamos datos de un Pie Chart, enumerar los números uno por uno es aburrido. Agrupa dos categorías y usa respectively (respectivamente) al final para conectar los nombres con los porcentajes en orden.

"Cars and public transport were the most popular modes, accounting for 60% and 25%, respectively."

Practice: Use fractions and "respectively"

(Data: Cars = 60%, Walking = 5%)
The percentages for cars and walking were 60% and 5% .
This means cars accounted for over (half/third) of the total, while walking was a small (fraction/double).

grid_on Lesson 2: Complex Tables & Extremes

Identifying Extremes (Superlatives)

En tablas con mucha información, NO debes describir cada celda. Debes buscar los extremos: the highest (el más alto), the lowest (el más bajo) o the most significant.

Practice: Superlative Structures

Looking at the table, London had (highest/higher) population growth in 2020. Conversely, Paris recorded the (lowest/lower) number of new residents during same period.
