star The STAR Method

Las universidades prestigiosas rara vez hacen preguntas de "sí o no". Utilizan preguntas conductuales (Behavioral Questions) como: "Tell me about a time you failed...". Para responder estructuradamente sin perderte en los detalles, usa el método STAR:

S - Situation

Establece el contexto. ¿Dónde estabas? ¿Cuál era el proyecto?
"During my final year of high school, my team and I had to present a science project..."

T - Task

¿Cuál era tu responsabilidad o el desafío a superar?
"We realized our data was corrupted just two days before the deadline..."

A - Action

Lo más importante. ¿Qué hiciste TÚ para resolverlo? Usa "I", no "We".
"I took the initiative to organize an emergency meeting and reassigned the research roles..."

R - Result

El final feliz y la lección aprendida.
"As a result, we not only finished on time but received the highest grade. It taught me the value of crisis management."

record_voice_over Power Phrases (B2/C1)

Evita frases básicas como "I think" o "I want to study here because it's good". Demuestra tu dominio del inglés usando este vocabulario estratégico:

To express strong passion/motivation:

  • "I have always been deeply passionate about..."
  • "My academic background has equipped me to..."
  • "I am highly driven by the prospect of..."

To connect your goals with the University:

  • "Your university’s commitment to [Value/Research] perfectly aligns with my personal goals."
  • "I was particularly drawn to your program because..."
  • "Receiving this scholarship would be a transformative opportunity for me to..."

Buying time to think (Instead of saying "Ehh..."):

  • "That is a very insightful question..."
  • "I'm glad you asked that. I would say that..."
  • "Let me reflect on that for a brief moment..."
