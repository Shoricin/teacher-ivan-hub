B2: Sustainable Tourism

Reading
Article: The Impact of Footprints.
Mass tourism provides economic benefits but poses significant environmental threats. Fragile ecosystems, such as coral reefs and alpine trails, are deteriorating due to excessive foot traffic. "Eco-tourism" has emerged as a solution, aiming to educate travelers and provide funds for ecological conservation, yet critics argue it is often just a marketing gimmick.
Listening

Listen to an interview snippet with an environmentalist.

play_arrow
Writing Task

Task: Write a short opinion piece (80-100 words). Do you believe Eco-Tourism is effective or just marketing? Why?

C1: Cultural Assimilation

Reading
Abstract: The Expat Dilemma.
Globalization has facilitated the movement of labor across borders, yet the psychological toll of cultural assimilation remains under-discussed. The phenomenon of 'culture shock' is not merely a transient phase but a profound restructuring of identity. Expatriates often find themselves in a liminal space, neither fully belonging to their host culture nor retaining the unaltered essence of their origin.
Listening

Listen to a lecture fragment on Sociology.

play_arrow
Writing Task

Task: Write a synthesis (100+ words). Discuss the challenges of living abroad mentioned in the text and audio. Add your perspective.
