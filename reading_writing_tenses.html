Experiences & Memories

Mastering Present Perfect vs. Past Simple
Present Perfect Life experiences and unfinished time (e.g., in my life, recently, so far).
Past Simple Finished actions at a specific time in the past (e.g., yesterday, in 2019).

menu_book Reading Example

My name is Alex, and I have loved traveling since I was a child. Over the past ten years, I have visited more than fifteen countries across three continents. I have seen the beautiful Eiffel Tower in Paris, and I have hiked through the dense jungles of Costa Rica. However, I have never been to Asia, but I hope to go there soon.

My absolute favorite adventure was my trip to Peru. Two years ago, I flew to Cusco with my best friend. We spent three days adjusting to the altitude, and then we started the Inca Trail. On the fourth day, we finally arrived at Machu Picchu. The sun was shining, and the view took my breath away. It is an experience I will never forget.
