There is / There are

Grammar Guide by Teacher Ivan

Affirmative +

To say something exists

We use There is for singular nouns (one thing) and There are for plural nouns (two or more).

Questions ?

Ask about existence

Change the order! Put the verb Is / Are first.

Is there...?
Yes, there is.
No, there isn't.
Are there...?
Yes, there are.
No, there aren't.
Practice:

_______ any students in the class?
