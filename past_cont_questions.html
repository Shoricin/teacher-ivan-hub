policy The Midnight Interrogation

Questions & Dramatic Interruptions

help_outline Past Continuous Questions

Para preguntar qué estaba sucediendo en un momento específico, invertimos el verbo To Be y el sujeto: (Wh-) + Was/Were + Subject + Verb(-ing)?
"What were you doing at 8 PM?"

bolt "Just As" (Dramatic Interruption)

Significa "Justo cuando". Lo usamos para enfatizar que dos eventos sucedieron en el mismo segundo exacto, creando mucha tensión dramática.
"Just as I was locking the door, the alarm went off."

The Art Gallery Heist

Detective Harris arrived at the National Art Gallery. The famous diamond necklace was missing, and the glass case was shattered. He immediately gathered the staff to start the interrogation.

"What were you doing when the alarm went off?" he asked the museum curator.

"I was organizing the new paintings in the basement," she answered nervously.

"Were you standing near the diamond at any point tonight?" Harris pressed.

"No, I wasn't. I was working far away in the East Wing."

Next, Harris interrogated the security guard. "Where were you looking at exactly midnight?"

"I was watching the security monitors. Everything was quiet and completely normal. But then, just as I was reaching for my cup of coffee, all the screens went completely black!" the guard explained, sweating.

"Was anyone else walking in the corridors?"

"Yes, the janitor was sweeping the floor near the entrance."

Harris found the janitor immediately. "What were you cleaning when the lights went out?"

"I was mopping the main hall. Just as I was finishing my shift, I heard a loud crash. I ran to the vault, but it was too late."

Harris smiled. He knew exactly who the thief was. "Just as the thief was escaping through the window, he made one crucial mistake... he dropped his silver watch." Harris held up the watch. "And this watch belongs to the guard."

Reading Comprehension

1. What was the curator doing when the alarm went off?

2. What happened "just as" the guard was reaching for his coffee?

3. Who was mopping the main hall?

4. How did Detective Harris know the guard was the thief?
