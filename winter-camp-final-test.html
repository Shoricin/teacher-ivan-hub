Lucas's Weekend Routine

My name is Lucas and I live in Sucre, Bolivia. During the week, my routine is very strict (estricta), but my weekends are different and fun. I usually wake up at 8:30 AM on Saturdays. I don't wear a uniform; I put on comfortable clothes like jeans and a t-shirt. First, I have a big breakfast with my family. We eat salteñas and drink orange juice. It is delicious!

After breakfast, I often help my parents with the chores (tareas del hogar). I clean my bedroom and take out the trash (basura). Then, in the afternoon, I meet my friends at the park. We love to play soccer or ride our bicycles. If the weather is rainy, we stay inside and play board games (juegos de mesa).

On Saturday evening, my family and I always have a special dinner. Sometimes we order pizza or go to a restaurant. Finally, I watch an action movie and go to bed at 11:00 PM. Sundays are for resting (descansar). I do my homework for school in the morning and prepare my backpack. I enjoy this time because I can relax and forget about exams. At night, I read a comic book before sleeping. I think having a good weekend routine is essential (esencial) for a healthy life.

2. What does he do after breakfast?
3. Where does he go with his friends in the afternoon?
4. What does Lucas do on Sunday mornings?
5. Why is his weekend routine important?
