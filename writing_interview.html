Vocabulary Helper

Use these words in your answers!

Reliable Confiable
Someone you can trust to work well.
"I am a reliable person, I never miss a deadline."
Background Experiencia/Fondo
Your past experience and education.
"I have a strong background in customer service."
Eager to learn Deseoso de aprender
Very enthusiastic about learning new things.
"I am eager to learn new software tools."
Hone my skills Perfeccionar habilidades
To sharpen or improve a specific skill.
"I want to hone my skills in graphic design."
Weakness Debilidad
An area where you need improvement.
"My weakness is public speaking, but I am practicing."
Long-term goal Meta a largo plazo
Something you want to achieve in the future.
"My long-term goal is to manage a team."

Interview Preparation Task

Answer the following questions using your own words. Try to incorporate the vocabulary from the left.

Tip: Talk about your current role, background, and one key achievement. Don't tell your life story.
Tip: Choose 2 or 3 skills relevant to the job. Use words like 'Reliable' or 'Organized'.
Tip: Be honest but mention how you are fixing it. (Don't say 'I work too hard').
Tip: Mention something specific about the company you like.
Tip: Connect your goals with the company's growth. Use 'Hone my skills'.
