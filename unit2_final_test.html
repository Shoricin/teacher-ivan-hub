history Narrative Tenses

  • Past Simple: Acciones principales que hacen avanzar la historia.
    "The engine stopped suddenly."
  • Past Continuous: Describir el fondo o una acción interrumpida.
    "We were sleeping when the alarm rang."
  • Past Perfect: Algo que sucedió ANTES de otra acción en el pasado.
    "I realized I had lost my passport."
  • Past Perfect Continuous: Una acción larga que ocurrió antes de otra en el pasado.
    "We had been walking for hours before we found water."

link Advanced Connectors

  • By the time: Para cuando. (Usa Past Perfect).
    "By the time we arrived, the plane had left."
  • As soon as: Inmediatamente después.
    "As soon as we landed, I called my mom."
  • Meanwhile: Mientras tanto.
    "I packed the bags; meanwhile, he called a taxi."
  • Eventually: Finalmente, a la larga.
    "After three delayed flights, we eventually arrived."

flight_takeoff Travel Vocabulary

  • Prepositions:
    IN a car/taxi (privado).
    ON a bus/plane/train/ship (público).
    BY car/bus (general).
  • Phrasal Verbs:
    Set off: Iniciar viaje.
    Take off: Despegar.
    Hold up: Retrasar.
    Get on/off: Subir/Bajar de un transporte público.
