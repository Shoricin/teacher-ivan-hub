🐕 Max and Snow's Winter Day

Max is a big brown dog, and Snow is a small white cat. They live with Teacher Ivan in a beautiful house. Today is a cold winter day. Max looks out the window. He sees white snow everywhere. He is happy. He wants to play outside. Snow the cat does not like the cold weather. She prefers to sleep near the warm fireplace.

At 8:00 AM, Ivan gives them breakfast. Max eats his delicious meat quickly. Snow eats her favorite fish slowly. After breakfast, Ivan puts on his red jacket and a blue scarf. He opens the door. "Let's go to the park, Max!" Ivan says. Max runs out fast. He loves to chase balls in the park.

At the park, Max runs and jumps. He meets other dogs. They are happy. Meanwhile, Snow is home. She wakes up from her nap. She walks to the kitchen. She sees a little toy mouse on the floor. She plays with it for twenty minutes.

At 12:00 PM, Ivan and Max return home. Max is tired but very happy. His paws are wet. Ivan cleans Max with a yellow towel. Snow looks at them from the sofa. Ivan prepares hot chocolate for himself and brings a small bowl of milk for Snow. Now, everyone is in the living room. Ivan reads a book, Max sleeps on the floor, and Snow sleeps on Ivan's lap. It is a perfect winter day for the family.

📅 Carlos's Winter Routine

During the winter vacation, Carlos changes his regular school routine to enjoy his free time, although he always tries to stay productive. He usually wakes up at 8:30 AM, which is much later than his usual 6:00 AM alarm. First, he stretches and stays in bed for a few minutes. Then, he logs into the Teacher Ivan HUB on his tablet to review his daily English challenges. He finds this digital method very engaging because it has zero traditional textbooks.

At 9:15 AM, Carlos goes downstairs to have breakfast. He usually prepares eggs, toast, and hot chocolate to fight the cold winter mornings. After that, he cleans his room and helps his parents with some household chores. Around 11:00 AM, his friend Liam usually picks him up in his car, and they go to the local library or a coffee shop to study together. They look forward to these meetings because they practice speaking English.

Carlos returns home at 1:30 PM for lunch. In the afternoon, he usually relaxes. He sometimes plays video games or watches a streaming series with English subtitles to improve his listening skills. However, he never forgets to check his assignments. At 4:00 PM sharp, he attends his presential Intermediate English Bootcamp. He loves the interactive role-plays and debates with his classmates. By 8:00 PM, he has dinner with his family, and they talk about their day. Finally, he goes to bed at 10:30 PM, feeling ready for the next winter adventure.
