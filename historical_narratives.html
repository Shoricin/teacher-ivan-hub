rule Time Prepositions

  • IN: Months, Years, Decades, Centuries.
    (e.g., in 1912, in July, in the 20th century)
  • ON: Specific Days and Dates.
    (e.g., on July 20th, on Monday)
  • DURING: A period of time (followed by a noun).
    (e.g., during the night, during the flight)
