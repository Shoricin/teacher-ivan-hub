English Conditionals

Didactic Guide by Teacher Ivan

Zero Conditional

Facts & General Truths

We use this for things that are always true, like scientific facts or rules.

If + Present Simple , Present Simple
"If you heat ice, it melts."
Science fact. 100% real.
Practice:

If it rains, the grass _____ (get) wet.

First Conditional

Real Possibilities (Future)

We use this for things that are likely to happen in the future if a condition is met.

If + Present Simple , Will + Verb
"If I study hard, I will pass the exam."
It's a real plan. It's possible.
Practice:

If she comes, I _____ (call) you.

Second Conditional

Unreal / Hypothetical

We use this for imaginary situations in the present or unlikely future. Dreams!

If + Past Simple , Would + Verb
"If I won the lottery, I would buy a house."
I didn't win. It's just imagination.
"If I were you, I would go."
Note: Use 'were' for all persons in formal English.
Practice:

If I _____ (have) money, I would travel.
