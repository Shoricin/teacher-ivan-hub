1. Sequencing & Present Passive (For Processes)

La fórmula es: Conector de secuencia + Is/Are + Verbo en Pasado Participio (V3).

(First/Then), the plastic bottles (are washed / washed). (Subsequently/Because), they (is crushed / are crushed) into small pieces.

2. Spatial Preps & Past Passive (For Maps)

La fórmula es: Ubicación + Objeto + Was/Were + Verbo en Pasado Participio (V3).

In the south, the old houses (was demolished / were demolished). They were replaced (by / for) a park. A new bridge (constructed / was constructed) (adjacent to / inside) the river.
