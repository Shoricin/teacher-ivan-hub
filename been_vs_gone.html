Distinguish between "Been" and "Gone"
BEEN (to)
Completed Trip (Return)
flight_takeoff ➔ place ➔ flight_land
Use been when someone went to a place and has already returned. It's a life experience.

• "I have been to Italy twice." (I am not in Italy now).
• "Has she ever been to Colegio Fielding?"
GONE (to)
One-Way Trip (Still there)
flight_takeoff ➔ ➔ ➔ place
Use gone when someone went to a place and has NOT returned yet. They are still there.

• "Where is Mark? He has gone to Spain." (He is in Spain right now).
• "My parents have gone to the supermarket."
