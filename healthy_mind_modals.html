psychology A Healthy Mind

Giving advice and expressing obligations

lightbulb Giving Advice

To say what is a good or bad idea.

  • Should / Shouldn't: "You should sleep more." (Deberías)
  • Ought to: "You ought to practice mindfulness." (Deberías - more formal)

gavel Obligations

To say what is necessary or prohibited.

  • Must: "I must change my habits." (Internal obligation)
  • Have to: "I have to wake up early for school." (External rule)
  • Mustn't: "You mustn't skip meals." (Prohibition)
