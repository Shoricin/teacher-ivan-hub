Mastering the difference between FOR and SINCE

FOR linear_scale

A Period of Time (Duration)
Use FOR to talk about an amount of time (how long something lasts). It can be used in different tenses.

• for 10 minutes
• for three weeks
• for a long time

Example: "I have lived in Sucre for 5 years."

SINCE place

A Point in Time (Starting Point)
Use SINCE to talk about a specific point in the past when an action started. It is mostly used with Perfect tenses.

• since 8:00 AM
• since Monday
• since I was a child

Example: "I have lived in Sucre since 2019."
