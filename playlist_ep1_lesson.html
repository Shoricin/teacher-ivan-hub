Episode 1: The Vision (Daniel Ek)

translate Essential Vocabulary

Before watching the episode, familiarize yourself with these key business and tech terms used by Daniel Ek and the developers.

1. Start-up (noun)

A newly established business, usually in the tech sector, designed to grow very quickly.

"Spotify began as a small start-up in Stockholm."

2. Buffering (noun)

The delay when a computer downloads a certain amount of data before playing a video or music.

"Daniel hated waiting for the music; he wanted zero buffering."

3. To Pitch (verb)

To present a business idea to an investor in order to get funding.

"He pitched his idea for a legal streaming service to Martin."

4. Piracy (noun)

The unauthorized and illegal copying or downloading of copyrighted content (like music or movies).

"Pirate Bay was the biggest piracy website in Sweden."

5. Royalties (noun)

Money paid to an artist or creator every time their work is played or sold.

"The record labels demanded high royalties for their songs."

6. Bandwidth (noun)

The maximum amount of data that can be sent over an internet connection in a given amount of time.

"They needed a lot of bandwidth to stream high-quality audio."
