Professions & Short Answers (Yes / No)

rule Grammar Rules: Short Answers

In English, we don't just say "Yes" or "No". We use short answers with the verb To Be.

✅ Affirmative (Yes)

  • Are you a student? ➔ Yes, I am.
  • Is he a doctor? ➔ Yes, he is.
  • Are they teachers? ➔ Yes, they are.

❌ Negative (No)

  • Are you a pilot? ➔ No, I'm not.
  • Is she a nurse? ➔ No, she isn't.
  • Are we singers? ➔ No, we aren't.
