precision_manufacturing Lesson 1: Describing Processes

Sequencing Connectors & Present Passive

Para describir procesos de manufactura (ej. cómo se hace el papel, el ciclo del agua), el objetivo del IELTS es evaluar tu capacidad para:

  • Ordenar los pasos: First, To begin with, Following this, Subsequently, Finally.
  • Usar la Voz Pasiva (Presente): Porque lo importante es el producto, no quién lo hace. La fórmula es: is / are + Verb (Past Participle).

"First, the raw materials are collected. Following this, they are processed in the factory."

Practice: The Recycling Process

(First/Although), the used plastic bottles (is collected / are collected) from the bins. (Subsequently / Whereas), they are transported to the recycling center where they (was washed / are washed) thoroughly.

map Lesson 2: Describing Maps

Spatial Prepositions & Past Passive

En el Task 1 a menudo te dan dos mapas de una ciudad (ej. 1990 vs 2020). Debes describir las transformaciones arquitectónicas:

  • Ubicación: To the north of, in the south-west corner, adjacent to, opposite.
  • Cambios (Past Passive): was demolished (destruido), was constructed/built (construido), was replaced by (reemplazado por).

"The old factory was demolished and it was replaced by a modern shopping mall."

Practice: Urban Transformations

Looking at the 2020 map, the old residential area in the south (was demolished / is demolished). It was replaced (for / by) a large park. Additionally, a new hospital (constructed / was constructed) (adjacent to / inside) the school.
