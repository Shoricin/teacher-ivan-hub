precision_manufacturing Lesson 1: Describing Processes

Sequencing Connectors & Present Passive

Cuando te toca describir un proceso de manufactura (ej. cómo se hace el chocolate) o un ciclo natural, tu objetivo es:

  • Ordenar los pasos lógicamente: Usa conectores como First, To begin with, Following this, Subsequently, Finally.
  • Usar la Voz Pasiva en Presente: En los procesos, lo importante es QUÉ le pasa al objeto, no QUIÉN lo hace. La fórmula es: is / are + Verb (Past Participle - V3).

"First, the raw materials are collected. Following this, they are processed in the factory."

Practice: The Tea Manufacturing Process

(First/Although), the tea leaves (is picked / are picked) by hand. (Subsequently / Whereas), they are transported to the factory where they (was dried / are dried) in the sun.

map Lesson 2: Describing Maps

Spatial Prepositions & Past Passive

En el Task 1 también pueden darte dos mapas de una misma zona (ej. la ciudad en 1990 vs 2020). Debes describir cómo se transformó el área usando:

  • Preposiciones Espaciales: To the north of, in the south-west corner, adjacent to, opposite.
  • Voz Pasiva en Pasado (Past Passive): Los cambios ya ocurrieron, por lo que usamos was / were + V3. Ejemplos: was demolished (destruido), was constructed/built (construido), was replaced by (reemplazado por), were planted (plantados).

"The old factory was demolished and it was replaced by a modern shopping mall."

Practice: Urban Transformations

Looking at the 2020 map, the old residential area in the south (was demolished / is demolished). It was replaced (for / by) a large park. Additionally, a new hospital (constructed / was constructed) (adjacent to / inside) the train station.
