Part 3: Reading Comprehension 20 Pts

Read the text about Sofia's routine and answer the questions below.

Sofia's Busy Day

My name is Sofia and I am a student in Bolivia. My daily routine is very busy but organized. I always wake up early, at 6:30 AM. First, I get up and take a quick shower. Then, I get dressed in my uniform and brush my teeth. At 7:00 AM, I have a healthy breakfast with my parents and my little brother. We usually eat bread, eggs, and drink hot chocolate.

After that, I take the bus to school. My classes start at 8:00 AM and finish at 1:00 PM. I study many subjects, but my favorite is English because I want to travel. I always have lunch at 1:30 PM at home. In the afternoon, I do my homework and study for my exams in the living room.

Sometimes, I play video games or watch TV for one hour to relax. I never go to bed late because I need energy for the next day. Finally, I have dinner at 7:30 PM, read a book, and go to sleep at 9:30 PM. During the weekend, my routine changes. I wake up late and play volleyball with my friends in the park.
1. What time does Sofia wake up on weekdays?
2. What does she usually eat for breakfast?
3. Why is English her favorite subject?
4. What does she do in the afternoon?
5. What does she do on the weekend?
